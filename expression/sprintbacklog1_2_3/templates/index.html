Mathematical Expression Solver

Welcome to Mathematical Expression Solver

It is specially designed for Solving the Mathematical Expression






To take multiple derivatives, pass the variable as many times as you wish to differentiate, or pass a number after the variable.
For example, diff(x**4, x, x, x)

To compute an indefinite integral just pass the variable after the expression. For example, integrate(cos(x), x)

To compute a definite integral, pass the argument (integration_variable, lower_limit, upper_limit). For example, integrate(exp(-x), (x, 0, oo))
